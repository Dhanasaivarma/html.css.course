Cricket Batting Styles

Aggressive Batting

Aggressive batting involves taking risks and playing attacking shots. Batsmen in this style often dominate bowlers and aim to score quickly.

Defensive Batting

Defensive batting focuses on protecting the wicket and playing safe shots. Batsmen in this style prioritize staying at the crease and wearing down bowlers.

Stylish Batting

Stylish batsmen emphasize elegance and flair in their shots. They play with finesse and focus on making their batting look aesthetically pleasing.

Power Hitting

Power hitters are known for their ability to clear the boundary regularly. They rely on strength and timing to hit the ball for maximum runs.
