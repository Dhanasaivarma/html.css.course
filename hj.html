Welcome to the World of Cricket

Cricket is a popular sport played around the world.

Rules of Cricket

  • Cricket is played between two teams.
  • Each team has 11 players.
  • The game consists of innings where both teams bat and bowl.
  • Runs are scored by hitting the ball and running between wickets.
  • Teams take turns to bat and bowl, and the goal is to score more runs than the opponent.

Famous Cricket Formats

  1. Test Cricket
  2. One Day International (ODI) Cricket
  3. T20 International Cricket

Famous Cricket Tournaments

  • ICC Cricket World Cup
  • ICC T20 World Cup
  • Indian Premier League (IPL)

Cricket Legends

  • Sachin Tendulkar
